Non-aligned and independent

MATUSA is not aligned to any particular political party and has a primary focus on worker issues.Members of MATUSA come from different political homes and what unites them is the need to fight the oppressive neo-liberal policies and programmes that are introduced by the employer.

Worker Controlled

MATUSA obtains its mandate from its members – the workers. This is done through democratic structures of the Union - shopfloor meetings, branch meetings, regional meetings, provincial meetings and national meetings.

Democratic

All union leaders (shopstewards and office bearers) are elected by members though a secret ballot. All important decisions of the union are balloted.

Corrupt free and accountable leader

MATUSA was formed as an alternative to a corrupt unions and union leadership. We believe that corruption compromises the union structures and union members. MATUSA has zero tolerance to corruption and will act decisively against corrupt leaders.

Militant

MATUSA’s strength lies in building a strong shopsteward (SS) movement that protects member participation in the union affairs and advances members’ interest in the workplace. MATUSA believes that a strong SS movement is highly dependent on a continuous training and education programme for members and elected representatives.

Non-racial and Non Sexist

MATUSA is a Union free of discrimination or unequal treatment with respect to race, gender, disability, creed, age or any arbitrary ground. MATUSA represents all its members equally.

Primary focus on workplace issues

MATUSA is a membership-based organization and its membership is made up mainly of workers. Our main aim is to protect and advance the interests of our members in the workplace. We do this by negotiate agreements with employers on pay and conditions of employment and representing them at the workplace and at the Bargaining Council as mandated by the Labour Relations Act, 1995.
