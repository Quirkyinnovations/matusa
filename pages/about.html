About MATUSA

Municipal and Allied Trade Union of South Africa (MATUSA). It was formed out of necessity to offer members satisfaction and accountability to Municipal and Allied workers within the Local Government Sector.

MATUSA is an independent trade union and genuinely controlled by workers, it has representatives and coordinators in all 9 provinces in South Africa.
